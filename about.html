About Us

Estela Pelico was the first owner of the farm, which she acquired in 1974. She started by growing bananas, beans and coffee. Our mission is to produce specialty coffee, maintaining environmentally friendly agricultural practices and focusing on corporate social responsibility.

For over two generations, the De Leon family has been envolving and improving on the enduring craft of organic Guatemalen coffee, combining Mexico signatures postres to create a blend of diversity that reflect the passion and the serving to our friends.
